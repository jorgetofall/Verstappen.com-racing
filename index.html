About Max Verstappen

Max Verstappen is a professional Formula 1 racing driver from the Netherlands, currently competing for the Oracle Red Bull Racing Team. Born on September 30, 1997, in Hasselt, Belgium, he is the son of former F1 driver Jos Verstappen. Max made his Formula 1 debut in 2015 with Scuderia Toro Rosso, becoming the youngest driver in F1 history at just 17 years old.

In 2016, he was promoted to Red Bull Racing and won his first Grand Prix in Spain, making history once again as the youngest race winner in the sport. Known for his aggressive driving style, technical skill, and relentless determination, Verstappen has rapidly become one of the most dominant figures in modern Formula 1.

Throughout his career, Max has broken numerous records and achieved multiple World Championship titles. His talent, consistency, and competitive spirit continue to captivate fans and reshape the future of Formula 1 racing.
